// Why Attend

This Is Your Invitation

The first-ever gathering of Sikh technologists, builders, and community leaders — all under one roof. Whether you write code, lead a Gurudwara, or simply care about the Panth's digital future, there's a seat for you.

⚙

The Panth's Best Builders. One Room.

Developers, designers, and visionaries already building for the community — finally in the same place. The network you've been missing.

❝

Ship Real Tools. Not Slide Decks.

Come with ideas, leave with working code. Hackathons where the output is software the Panth can actually use — starting that day.

❖

Set the Standard for AI & Gurbani

AI will handle Gurbani whether we're at the table or not. Be the ones who define how — with accuracy, reverence, and zero compromise.

⧉

Build Once. Share Everywhere.

Too many teams solving the same problems alone. Open-source means we stop duplicating effort and start multiplying impact.
